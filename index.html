Our Mission

The Smith Family supports disadvantaged children by providing them with the educational resources and support necessary so they can participate more fully in society.





Currently 1 in 6 Australian children are living below the poverty line.


Disadvantaged children are on average 2-3 years behind their peers from higher socio-economic positions.


By 2033, The Smith Family hopes to reach 30% more disadvantaged Australian children with our learning programs, to ensure they break the cycle of disadvantage and are provided with the same opportunities as their peers.
