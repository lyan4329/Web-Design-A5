Our Mission

Our purpose is to create opportunities for young Australians in need by providing long-term support for their participation in education.


We want to help children and youth break the cycle of disadvantage so they can achieve their full potential in school and the workforce.



Our Impact

  • Our programs reached 206,836 participants in 2018-19, up 18% in comparison to the previous year

  • We celebrated 66.7% of Learning for Life students advancing from Year 10 to Year 12 in 2018.

  • 88% of our Learning for Life tertiary students continued from first year to second year University study.
